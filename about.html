About EduSync

EduSync is a web-based platform designed to help students and teachers manage
group projects effectively. Our mission is to make collaboration, task tracking,
and project management simple and intuitive.

With EduSync, teachers can assign projects, monitor progress, and review
submissions — while students can coordinate tasks, track milestones, and
submit their work easily. The platform encourages teamwork, accountability,
and smooth progress.

EduSync aims to make group projects stress-free and organized, allowing every
team member to contribute efficiently toward success.
